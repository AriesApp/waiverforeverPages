Update log:

3.0.0 September 1, 2017

After months of hard work, we’re proud to deliver our new version!

We have redesigned the entire information structure to bring many handy features from the web to the app for free.

In the new version, we explicitly divided the app into two parts: the app console and kiosk.

The kiosk is the old friend you’re already familiar with. The app console is like your best friend, who can help you deal with your everyday tasks on the go.

There are four new tabs in the app console:

  • Dashboard: view devices, templates, and waiver usage.
  • Templates: all your templates and signed waivers on the cloud
  • Account: profile and settings
  • More: about, help center, and support.
